Here's a timeline of Brandon's life:


  • 1965 - Born in Oakland, California

  • 1973 - Moved back to California after his father's passing and started to reintroduce martial arts in his life at 9 years old with Dan Inosanto (Bruce Lees's former student and training partner)

  • 1983 - As a student he was quite rebellious which led him to leave the Chadwick high school. He also changed direction from training martical arts to play soccer during this period, one speculated reason for that is that the dojos he had to train in were filled with posters of his father which naturally bothered him. Including that he seemed to have a inner conflict with his identity

  • 1984 - Brandon moved to New York after he earned his GED from Miraleste high school, in New York he began to study at Lee Strasberg Theatre and Film Institute. He eventually went to Emerson College as he majored in theatre

  • 1986 - As he'd finished his studies, he made a number of stage productions at the Eric Morris American New Theatre. This is the start of Brandon's career as he began to work as a script reader, but soon enough got to be starring in movies and tv series such as Kung Fu, Kung Fu: The Movie, Legacy of Rage, Ohara, Laser Mission and more. He got nominated for a Hong Kong film award for Best New Performer of the role he played in "Legacy of Rage"

  • 1990 - Meanwhile, Brandon meets his future fiancée, Eliza (Liza) Hutton

  • 1991 - Eliza and Brandon move in together. As he continues to work with a few upcoming movies, (Showdown in Little Tokyo, Rapid Fire and Sex, Lögner och videovåld).

  • 1992 - The couple got engaged and Brandon got the role of Eric Draven in The Crow, which would be his last movie

  • 1993 - Brandon dies at 28 years old, his death was caused by a fatal injury during the filming of The Crow. The tragic accident occured on set while filming the scene of Brandon's character, Eric Draven, getting brutally murdered by a gang of criminals. The actor Michael Massee that played as one of the gang members, shot at Brandon with a loaded gun of blank rounds. They had neglect to clean the pistol for this scene, so a fragment from the bullet from a dummy round which had previously been used for close-ups, flew and hit Brandon in the abdomen

  • 1994 - The movie got finished with Tchad Stahelski as a stunt double and Jeff Cadiente as a stand-in, with the help of CGI they could replicate Brandond's facial features on Jeff. The movie is dedicated to Brandon and Eliza
